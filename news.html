Camera Traps Spot New Tiger Cubs in Taman Negara — A Rare Sign of Hope

PERHILITAN conservationists have confirmed three new Malayan Tiger cubs captured on camera trap footage deep within Taman Negara National Park. The discovery marks the first recorded birth in the area in over three years, offering a rare glimmer of hope for Malaysia's critically endangered national symbol. Researchers credit improved anti-poaching patrols and the expansion of the Royal Belum-Temengor forest complex as key factors in this success.
